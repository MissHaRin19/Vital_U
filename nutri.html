Check Out The Tasty Healthy Recipies

UppmaDry roast 1 cup of rava (semolina) in a pan on low to medium heat until crunchy.Keep stirring often for even roasting. In another pan, heat ghee and add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves, and green chilies.Sauté until the mustard seeds pop. Add 2.5 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring to a boil. Slowly add the roasted rava while stirring continuously. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the upma thickens
Rava, Ghee, Mustered seeds, urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves, green chilies
KichidiIn a pan, dry roast 1 cup of rice and 1 cup of moong dal until fragrant. Add 4 cups of water, salt to taste, and pressure cook for 3 whistles. Once the pressure is released, add 1 tablespoon of ghee and serve hot
1 cup rice , 1 cup moong dal, Salt to taste , 1 tablespoon ghee
Palak PaneerIn a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil and sauté 2 green chilies and 3/4 cup of finely chopped onions until the onions turn translucent. Add 3.5 to 4 cups of spinach (palak) and cook until wilted. Let it cool and blend into a smooth puree. In another pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add the spinach puree, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes and add 1.25 cups of cubed paneer. Cook for another 2-3 minutes and serve hot
150 grams (1¼ cups), paneer (Indian cottage cheese), 3½ to 4 cups (100 to 120 grams) , palak (spinach), 2 tablespoons oil (or half oil & half butter), 2 green chilies (deseeded) (less spicy kind) , ¾ cup (90 grams, 1 small) , onions (fine chopped) , ½ teaspoon cumin seeds, ½ teaspoon salt, ¼ teaspoon garam masala
Allu ParootaBoil 3 medium-sized potatoes until fork-tender. Peel and mash them with 1 green chili, 2 tablespoons of finely chopped coriander leaves, 3/4 teaspoon of grated ginger, 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon of garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of Kashmiri chili powder. In a pan, heat oil and sauté 1/2 cup of finely chopped onions until they turn translucent. Add the mashed potato mixture and cook for 2-3 minutes. Divide the dough into small balls, stuff each ball with the potato mixture, and roll it out into a thin parotta. Cook the parotta on a hot griddle with oil or ghee until golden brown.
3 medium-sized potatoes, 1 green chili, 2 tablespoons of finely chopped coriander leaves, 3/4 teaspoon of grated ginger, 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon of garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of Kashmiri chili powder, 1/2 cup of finely chopped onions, Oil or ghee for cooking,
